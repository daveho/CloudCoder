Enter the hostname that you will use for your CloudCoder instance.

If you would like to use No-IP to provide a DNS hostname for your CloudCoder instance, check the Use No-IP checkbox, and then enter your No-IP username and password. Note that the hostname must belong to one of the domains that No-IP offers, e.g. ddns.net. No-IP is a free service to provide DNS hostnames for devices whose IP address may change dynamically. Your CloudCoder instance's IP address won't actually change, but No-IP is an easy way to get a DNS hostname without registering a domain, configuring a nameserver, etc.

If you do not choose to use No-IP, then you will need to configure an A record for the hostname you choose. The wizard will let you know what IP address to use once the webapp server has been provisioned.
